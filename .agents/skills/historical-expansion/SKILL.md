# Historical Expansion Skill

## Goal

Transform a historical topic into a rich interconnected historical universe.

---

## Required Expansion Layers

For every historical topic, expand:

1. Core Event
2. Main Characters
3. Family Relationships
4. Political Factions
5. Institutional Background
6. Geographic Importance
7. Historical Causes
8. Immediate Consequences
9. Long-term Consequences
10. Related Idioms
11. Related Historical Events
12. Future Historical Echoes
13. Parallel Historical Examples

---

## Mandatory Questions

Always ask:

- Why did this happen?
- Who benefited?
- Who feared it?
- What changed afterward?
- What hidden relationship mattered?
- What future event became possible because of this?

---

## Output Requirements

Generate:
- markdown summary
- relationship graph
- timeline
- historical significance
- dramatic story nodes

---

## Forbidden

Do NOT:
- summarize only surface facts
- produce textbook-style chronology
- isolate events from historical context
