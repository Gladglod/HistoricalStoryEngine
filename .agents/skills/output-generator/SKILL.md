# Ordinary Reader Skill

## Audience

Assume:
- reader knows very little history
- reader has low patience
- reader prefers stories over facts

---

## Writing Rules

Use:
- short paragraphs
- simple explanations
- vivid storytelling
- emotional framing
- concrete examples

---

## Forbidden

Avoid:
- academic language
- dense terminology
- large date blocks
- excessive names without explanation
- long political exposition

---

## Reader Retention

Every few paragraphs include:
- conflict
- surprise
- irony
- human emotion
- shocking historical connection
