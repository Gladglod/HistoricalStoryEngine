# Narrative Hooks Skill

## Opening Rule

The first paragraph MUST create:

- danger
- mystery
- irony
- emotional tension
- political crisis
- betrayal
- impossible choices

---

## Hook Types

Use:

- "The day everything changed"
- "The hidden truth behind..."
- "The man who almost destroyed..."
- "Why nobody noticed..."
- "The decision that doomed..."
- "The betrayal that reshaped..."

---

## Curiosity Loop

Every section should end with:
- a hidden question
- an unresolved conflict
- a surprising implication

---

## Forbidden

Do NOT:
- open with dates
- open with dry historical introductions
- explain everything immediately
