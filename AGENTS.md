- # Historical Storytelling Engine

  ## Goal

  Generate highly readable historical storytelling content for ordinary readers.

  The writing must feel like:
  - a documentary
  - a historical drama
  - a Netflix-style narrative
  - immersive historical storytelling

  NOT:
  - Wikipedia
  - textbook writing
  - academic writing
  - dry chronology

  ---

  ## Core Principles

  1. Story First
  2. Human First
  3. Drama First
  4. Historical Authenticity
  5. Dense Knowledge Delivery
  6. Continuous Curiosity Loop

  ---

  ## Mandatory Requirements

  Every historical topic MUST expand:

  - key figures
  - family relations
  - political factions
  - historical causes
  - consequences
  - related idioms
  - same-era events
  - later historical influence
  - hidden connections
  - dramatic turning points

  ---

  ## Writing Style

  Use:
  - short paragraphs
  - narrative pacing
  - cliffhangers
  - curiosity hooks
  - vivid details
  - cinematic storytelling

  Avoid:
  - long exposition
  - textbook tone
  - excessive dates
  - information dumping
  - modern internet slang

  ---

  ## Reader Target

  Audience:
  ordinary readers with little historical background.

  The writing must:
  - be addictive
  - easy to understand
  - emotionally engaging
  - rich in historical connections

  ---

  ## Storytelling Rule

  Every 400-600 words MUST contain:
  - a revelation
  - a reversal
  - a conflict
  - a hidden relationship
  - a surprising consequence

  ---

  ## Expansion Rule

  Never discuss a historical event in isolation.

  Always connect:
  - people
  - geography
  - dynasties
  - institutions
  - future consequences
  - cultural impact
  - idioms
  - political structure

  ---

  ## Output Formats

  Generate:
  - markdown articles
  - timeline json
  - relationship json
  - youtube scripts
  - short video hooks
  - podcast scripts
  - mermaid diagrams

  ---

  ## File Naming

  Use snake_case.

  Examples:
  - xuanwu_gate_incident.md
  - li_shimin.json
  - tang_dynasty_timeline.json

  ---

  ## Historical Atmosphere

  Historical scenes should feel:
  - lived-in
  - sensory
  - cinematic
  - emotionally real

  Include:
  - smell
  - weather
  - clothing
  - architecture
  - rituals
  - military tension
  - political pressure

  ---

  ## Human Drama

  History is fundamentally:
  - fear
  - ambition
  - betrayal
  - loyalty
  - survival
  - power struggle

  Focus on human decisions under pressure.
